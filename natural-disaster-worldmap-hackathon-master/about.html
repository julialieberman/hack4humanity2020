Mission

Global Disaster Relief was founded on March 3, 2018 with the goal of publicizing natural disaster and raising awareness on the efforts undertaken my selfless individuals to save lives. Through this website, Global Disaster Relief hopes to encourage people to contribute and take part in organizations dedicated with the prevention, reponse, and recovery to catastrophies. Make sure that show your support for those in need and to thank those who go out of their way to make the world a safer, happier place.


Support

The best way to get involved is to...get involved! Take a loop at our global disaster map and check out a few of the data points. They are updated everyday give useful information on ways in which you can get support organizations arond the globe that are working around the clock to provide people with food, water, electricity, housing, and safety. Don't be shy, you are one click away from saving a life!
